About Sarthak Pet Store

At Sarthak Pet Store, we are passionate about pets and dedicated to providing the best products and services to keep your pets happy and healthy. Whether you're looking for food, toys, grooming supplies, or medical care, we've got you covered with a wide range of quality pet products.

Founded with the belief that pets are family, our store has grown to become a trusted name in the pet industry. Our team is committed to helping pet owners find the right products for their pets, offering expert advice, and ensuring every pet gets the care they deserve.

Our Mission

We strive to be a one-stop shop for all your pet needs, offering a convenient shopping experience both online and in-store. Our mission is to make pet care easier, more affordable, and more accessible to all pet owners.
